About Our Hotel

Nestled in a serene location, Hotel Everest offers an unforgettable escape from the ordinary. Our commitment to world-class hospitality, combined with a tranquil environment, makes us the perfect destination for both leisure and business travelers. From our luxurious rooms to our exceptional dining experiences, every detail is crafted for your comfort and satisfaction.

Founded in 1985, Hotel Everest has a rich history of providing impeccable service. We pride ourselves on our friendly staff, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedication to creating memorable moments for every guest.
